Rib Steak

Ingredients:

  • rib steak - 400g
  • salt - 1 pincée
  • olive oil - 2 tablespoon
  • garlic clove - 1 large
  • thyme - 1 branch

Steps:

  1. Melt the butter and oil mixture to have a very hot pan before placing the meat. Place the meat on the very hot pan and move it around so it doesn't stick.

  2. Place a sprig of thyme or herbs on the meat while cooking the first side.

  3. Depending on the thickness of the meat and the desired cooking level (rare, medium rare, cooked), cook for 2 to 10 minutes per side. In general, 5 minutes is sufficient.

  4. Once the desired cooking level is reached, let it rest for 5 minutes under aluminum foil to let the meat relax while keeping it warm before serving.

Lentils with carrots

Ingredients:

  • green lentils - 400g
  • carrot - 1
  • onion - 2

Steps:

  1. Peel the onions, ginger and garlic cloves and chop them. Peel the carrot and cut it into large cubes.

  2. Sauté the chopped onions until they become translucent. Add garlic and ginger, but not too much...otherwise their taste could be distracting. Now add the lentils and carrot cubes. Cover with water and simmer for 30 minutes.

  3. Now add the not-so-secret final ingredient: balsamic vinegar. Cook until the liquid has been completely absorbed. This dish goes with just about everything. And you won’t go wrong serving it with coleslaw…
