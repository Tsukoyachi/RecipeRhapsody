Steps:

  1. Melt the butter and oil mixture to have a very hot pan before placing the meat. Place the meat on the very hot pan and move it around so it doesn't stick.

  2. Place a sprig of thyme or herbs on the meat while cooking the first side.

  3. Depending on the thickness of the meat and the desired cooking level (rare, medium rare, cooked), cook for 2 to 10 minutes per side. In general, 5 minutes is sufficient.

  4. Once the desired cooking level is reached, let it rest for 5 minutes under aluminum foil to let the meat relax while keeping it warm before serving.
